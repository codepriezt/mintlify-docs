To start using Cloudsania, follow these steps:

### **Step 1: Create a Cloudsania account**

1. Visit [**Cloudsania 'Get Started'** Page](https://console.cloudsania.com/auth/sign-up)

2. Fill in the following details:

   * **First Name**

   * **Last Name**

   * **Email**

   * **Password** *(Must include at least 8 characters, one special character, one uppercase letter, and a number)*

3. Agree to the [**Terms of Service**](https://cloudsania.com/terms) **and** [**Privacy Policy**](https://cloudsania.com/terms#privacy).

4. Click **Register**.

📌 *After registering, you will be redirected to the email verification page.*


## **2️⃣  Verify Your Email**

Before accessing Cloudsania console, you need to verify your email.

### **Step 1: Check Your Email**

* You’ll receive an email with a **6-digit** **verification code**.

* If you don’t see it, check your spam folder.

* Click **"Enter Code Manually"**.


### **Step 2: Verify Your Email**

* Enter the **verification code** sent to your email.

* Click **Continue**.

✅ If successful, you’ll see a confirmation message.


## **3️⃣ Setting Up Your Organization**

After verifying your email, you will be asked to provide **your organization details**.

### **Step 1: Enter Your Organization Details**

1. **Organization Name**

2. **Organization Address**

3. **Organization Email**

4. **Phone Number**

Click **Continue** to finalize setup.

📌 *This step is required to manage cloud services and team collaborations.*


## **4️⃣ Choosing a Plan**

Once your organization is set up, select the pricing plan that best fits your organization's needs.

### **Step 1: Select Your Pricing Plan**

* The **Free Plan** includes:✅ Access to all basic features✅ Multiple **Konstacks**✅ Up to **5 users**✅ Unlimited Projects✅ Cloud Connectors✅ Built-in DNS Configurations✅ Up to **3 Add-ons per Environment**

* Click **"Get Started"** to proceed.


## **5️⃣ Confirmation & Welcome Message**

After selecting your plan, you will see a confirmation message indicating successful account creation.

### **Step 1: Review Confirmation**

* A pop-up will appear stating that your account has been successfully created and subscribed to your preferred Plan.

* Click **"Get Started"** to proceed to the home page.


### **Congratulations 🎊🎊🎊 and welcome to Cloudsania**!

## **6️⃣ Exploring the Home Page**

After selecting your plan, you’ll land on the **Cloudsania Home Page**, where you can start setting up.

### **Step 1: Welcome Message & Quick Start Actions**

On the **Home Page**, you’ll see a **Welcome Message** and **Quick Start Options** to help you get started faster.

### **Step 2: Quick Start Actions**

From the home page, you can:

1. [**Connect your cloud service provider**](https://developer.cloudsania.com/cloudServiceProviders/cloudProviders)

2. [**Integrate your code repository**](https://developer.cloudsania.com/integrations/github)

3. [**Deploy a new project**](https://developer.cloudsania.com/projects/projectMode)

4. **Invite your team members**

5. **Complete your KYC**

6. **Add new integrations**

📌 *Click on any of these options to begin setup!*
