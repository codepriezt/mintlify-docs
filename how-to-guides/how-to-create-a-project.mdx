## Overview

This guide will walk you through the step-by-step process of creating a [Project](/projects/project-in-cloudsania) using our three specialized project modes, each designed to cater to your project's uniqueness and different expertise levels.

<Note>
  Choose the project mode that best matches your team's expertise and project requirements. You can always start with Easy Mode and transition to more advanced modes as your needs evolve.
</Note>
 
<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Easy Mode">
Perfect for beginners or those seeking quick deployment. It offers streamlined [App Services](/projects/app-service) with pre-configured [Konstacks](https://docs.cloudsania.com/environments/konstacks), allowing you to deploy your project without deep cloud knowledge.

    #### How to Create a Project Using Easy Mode
    1. Log in to your [**Cloudsania**](https://console.cloudsania.com/auth/sign-in) account.
    2. Click on "Projects" from the side nav bar.
    3. Click on 'New Project'.

    ![](https://api.monosnap.com/file/download?id=4AYL6HFhygrRvl9Tbr9piPJ0RAyWoD)

    4. In the pop-up form, enter your Project name and Project Decription.
    5. Click on the "Confirm" button to proceed.

    ![](https://docs.cloudsania.com/~gitbook/image?url=https%3A%2F%2F1443322201-files.gitbook.io%2F%7E%2Ffiles%2Fv0%2Fb%2Fgitbook-x-prod.appspot.com%2Fo%2Fspaces%252F9D0JTnpRisktkAiaEL7H%252Fuploads%252FBgfjhWmoMgCDMJIS9493%252Fimage.png%3Falt%3Dmedia%26token%3D0b9ee643-9a0a-4943-a13a-997312b78b68\&width=768\&dpr=4\&quality=100\&sign=324edacd\&sv=2)

    6. Select "Easy Mode" from the list of Project modes
    7. Click on the "Confirm" button

    ![](https://api.monosnap.com/file/download?id=a0uLsNlK0i5msWP1xZvX2qugoBn0gG)

    8. Upon successful creation, your project will be displayed on the Projects page.
    ![](https://api.monosnap.com/file/download?id=4DURjRj1up0sWAfLuqvPyYC1296y1R)
