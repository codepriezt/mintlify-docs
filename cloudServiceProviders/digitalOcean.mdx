## üîó**Step 3: Authorize Cloudsania to Access Your DigitalOcean Account**

Before Cloudsania can manage your DigitalOcean resources, you must authorize the application via **OAuth Authentication**.

### **Step 3.1: Log in to DigitalOcean**

1. Open a new tab and go to [**DigitalOcean**]().

2. Enter your **DigitalOcean credentials** and log in.

### **Step 3.2: Navigate to OAuth Applications**

1. In your **DigitalOcean dashboard**, go to **API** in the left navigation panel.

2. Click on the **OAuth Applications** tab.

3. Click **Register a New Application**.

### **Step 3.3: Register Cloudsania as an OAuth Application**

To allow Cloudsania to interact with your DigitalOcean account securely, you need to register it as an OAuth application.

1. Click **Register New Application**.

2. Enter the following details:

   * **Application Name:** Cloudsania

   * **Redirect URL:** [`https://console.cloudsania.com/auth/callback`](https://console.cloudsania.com/auth/callback)

   * **Permissions:** Select the required permissions for Cloudsania to manage your resources (e.g., read, write, and deploy resources).

3. Click **Save and Generate Credentials**.

### **Step 3.4: Copy OAuth Credentials**

Once the application is registered, DigitalOcean will generate OAuth credentials for you.

1. Copy the **Client ID** and **Client Secret** generated for the Cloudsania application.

2. Store them securely, as they will be required in Cloudsania.

## üîó**Step 4: Enter Connector Details in Cloudsania**

1. Return to **Cloudsania**.

2. Enter the **Connector Name** and **Account Name**.

3. Click **Continue**.

![](https://api.monosnap.com/file/download?id=Xb2prRoYN83wEZV1lsBaROqRGAiaCf)

## üîó**Step 5: Configure Connector Access**

1. Select OAuth Authentication (Recommended).

2. Paste the **Client ID** and **Client Secret** from DigitalOcean.

3. Click 'Create'  and you will be redirected to Digital Ocean to authorize Cloudsania access.&#x20;

![](https://api.monosnap.com/file/download?id=pLkimqzaH92jfjqJOmZwKGk2E8GlHb)

## üîó**Step 6: Authorize Cloudsania Access**

1. When prompted, review the permissions requested by Cloudsania.

2. Ensure that '**Read'** and '**Write'** permissions are enabled.

3. Select the team that should have access.

4. Click **Authorize Authorization** to grant access.

5. If successful, your DigitalOcean account will be linked to Cloudsania

6. You will be redirected back to the **Cloudsania app,** where you can view your newly created Digital Ocean Connector.
