## Welcome to Cloudsania Documentation

Here you'll find everything you need to master our cloud deployment and management platform. Whether you're deploying your first application or managing complex multi-cloud infrastructure, this documentation will guide you through every step of your journey.
## What is Cloudsania?

Cloudsania is a DevOps automation platform designed to streamline the deployment and management of infrastructures and applications across multiple cloud providers. It simplifies cloud complexities, enabling users to set up, configure, and deploy applications efficiently. Our platform enables you to:
